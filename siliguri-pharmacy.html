Project Context

About Siliguri Pharmacy

Siliguri Pharmacy is an on-demand medical delivery platform enabling local residents to browse medications, consult specialists, and receive healthcare goods at home. I acted as the UX Specialist, focusing intensely on inclusive design and accessibility parameters.

The Challenge

What Was Broken

Unlike general consumer e-commerce platforms, an online medical pharmacy is predominantly utilized by elderly users, anxious caregivers, or sick individuals. Complex navigation loops, tiny text sizes, and ambiguous prescription upload buttons caused significant user frustration.

UX/UI Solutions

How I Solved It

01
Strict WCAG Accessibility Alignment

Used large font hierarchies, clear field focus states, and high-contrast styling to assist users with low vision or hand tremors.

02
Streamlined Prescription Upload Interface

Designed a prominent, drag-and-drop prescription submission modal with clear helper text and file status tracking, reducing data errors by 40%.

03
Simplified Checkout Path

Created a streamlined checkout funnel featuring large touch targets, automated address mapping, and plain-language order notifications.
